In maths, I started with set theory and logic, but lately I'm trying out other fields as well. I sometimes write blogs about what I find, here, or on my Tumblr. My favourite number is $\mathfrak{d}$. It's called the dominating number, it's defined as the minimal number of functions on $\mathbb{N}$ needed to eventually surpass all other functions. In symbols, $\mathfrak{d} := \min \{ \# S \mid S \subset \mathbb{N}^{\mathbb{N}} \wedge \forall f: \mathbb{N} \to \mathbb{N} \ \exists g \in S \ \forall^{\infty} n. f(n) \le g(n) \}$.
